Emailing sensitive information

You are responsible for the information you share. If you need to email information that might pose a risk if lost or made public, like case notes or medical data, you must:

  • share it on a ‘need to know’ basis
  • mark email so the recipients know how to handle it
  • check the domain you are sending to is on the list below

If the domain is listed use your normal corporate email account - your email will be sent securely

If the domain is not listed use the email encryption portal instead.

These domains have enabled anti-spoofing and encryption, and have passed an email assessment, in line with the guidance on securing government email. You can use them to create routing or compliance rules, or to help decide whether to send an email to a particular recipient.
